Open-world prototype

Street Heat

A lightweight GTA-style top-down game. Steal cars, evade police, grab cash, and clear short missions in a compact city sandbox.

Controls

  • MoveWASD / Arrows
  • SprintShift
  • ShootSpace
  • Enter / Exit carE
  • Restart after bustR

Goals

  • Mission 1Collect 3 cash drops
  • Mission 2Escape police at 2 heat
  • Mission 3Jack a moving car

Rules

Shooting and hitting civilians raise heat. Police spawn harder as heat climbs. Lose the cops by staying out of their detection radius long enough.

Cash $0
Heat 0
Health 100
Vehicle On Foot
Mission Collect 3 cash drops scattered across the city.
Cruise the city and start trouble.
